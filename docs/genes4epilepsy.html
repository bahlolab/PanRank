PanRank - Genes4Epilepsy

Jacob E. Munro, Mark F. Bennett, Melanie Bahlo

Genes4Epilepsy is a curated list of monogenic epilepsy genes.

This page contains PanRank candidate gene rankings based on gene panels from Genes4Epilepsy. PanRank scores are between 0 and 1 and represent the predicted probability that a gene is disease-causing with the specified mode of inheritance. The “Inheritance” column indicates the known mode of inheritance used to train the model.

Note: “GGE” and “Focal” phenotypes have insufficient numbers of recessively inherited genes for training so no predictions are made.
